Cloud Governance Services

Business Justification

In the dynamic world of cloud computing, governance is paramount to ensure regulatory compliance, cost efficiency, and secure operations. Cloud Governance in the CloudForge portfolio aids EPAM projects in achieving their objectives while effectively managing risks associated with cloud infrastructures.

Supported Activities

  • Planning and alignment of cloud resources with business objectives
  • Cloud cost management and optimization
  • Compliance monitoring and enforcement in line with EPAM Cloud Security Policies

Desired Outcomes in Terms of Utility

The EPAM projects can manage, deploy, and scale cloud resources with clear guidelines, without being constrained by compliance concerns or inefficiencies.

Desired Outcomes in Terms of Warranty

EPAM projects are assured of a secure, compliant, and cost-optimized cloud environment, ensuring business continuity and safeguarding organizational reputation.

Developer Enablement Services

Business Justification

A competent and continuously improving development team is a critical asset. Developer Enablement ensures that the EPAM projects are equipped with the services, translating into faster time-to-market, higher product quality, and enhanced innovation capabilities.

Supported Activities

  • Centralized artifact storage for better accessibility and version control for development assets
  • Streamlined code management, fostering a collaborative and efficient development process
  • Systematic review processes to improve code quality and reduce defects
  • Acceleration of software release cycles with automation service in line with CI/CD practices

Desired Outcomes in Terms of Utility

The EPAM projects are equipped with the right services, enabling them to efficiently manage, develop, test, and deploy software, freeing them from manual processes and letting innovation thrive.

Desired Outcomes in Terms of Warranty

All services are reliable, up-to-date, and aligned with the best practices of modern software development.
